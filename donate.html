Support the World Literacy Foundation

We want Epistle Literary to be as accessible as possible, so donations will never be required for submission. However, we strongly encourage and deeply appreciate anything you can offer. 100% of your donations go towards the World Literacy Foundation in support of their mission to end illiteracy worldwide. Every amount helps—

  • $25 USD gives one child a literacy pack, which contains a new book, a literacy workbook, stationary, and literacy resources for their entire family.
  • $75 USD goes toward building a pop-up library in an underprivileged area so that a child can pick out a new favorite story for free.
  • $150 USD could help train a teacher in the unique skills necessary for promoting literacy in an off-the-grid classroom. If you're like us, you believe in the power that a single educator has to create change—help us empower one!
Donate with WLF

When donating, please be sure to enter Rory Baskin as the ambassador you are supporting! This will be a required step for you to submit the donation form.
