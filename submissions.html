Submit Your Work

We accept any form of writing, so long as it responds to the theme: "How has an educator or mentor shaped you?" Consider poetry, short fiction, creative nonfiction, and letters to honor your educators.

  • Up to 3 poems (max 5 pages total)
  • Short stories up to 3,000 words
  • Essays, memoirs, or letters up to 2,000 words

Please send your submission to submissions@epistleliterary.org as a PDF. Submissions not attached as PDFs will not be opened for consideration.
