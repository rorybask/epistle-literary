Welcome to Epistle Literary Magazine

Epistle Literary Magazine is a love letter to educators everywhere. Proceeds from all issues support the World Literacy Foundation, a global nonprofit aiming to improve the lives of children everywhere through literacy. We accept poetry, short fiction, essays, and letters. We publish emerging and established voices and are committed to showcasing a wide range of styles and perspectives.
